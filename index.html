About Accessible Design Co.

Accessible Design Co. was founded in 2025 when an instructor gave their class an assignment - to make an awesomely accessible website!
Accepting this challenge, a intrepid team of developers founded this company and was about to build a website when they were all sadly eaten by a grue. Fortunately, the grue read the documentation and was inspired to start a career in web development!
